Power 2 Start


Entrepreneurship Resources for Youth

Welcome to Power2start. This website is for young Irish based entrepreneurs and future entrepreneurs (15-24 years old) who have an idea or project they would like to develop further. The website is designed to empower young people with the knowledge, skills and resources they may need to bring their idea to life. Developing a support network is important for entrepreneurship to thrive and the Power2start community page has been designed with this in mind.

We are Emma O'Brien, a PhD Researcher

and Diana Martin, a PhD Researcher at Dublin Institute of Technology, co-founder of the NGO Link Education and Practice, named in 2015 by the Forum Alpbach as a European innovator in tackling inequality in education
